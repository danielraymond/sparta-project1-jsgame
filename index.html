How to play:

This game involves making decisions depending on the text the game presents you with.
First you need to set your health which you will find displayed in the top left of the screen. This is done by clicking a button when prompted.
Next the game will present you with a block of text and you must decide what to do based on the options given.
At some point in the game you will reach a combat scenario. To win you will need to click the button which rolls dice where the difference in values is equal to the damage either you or the enemy takes.
There are items in the game that have various effects that may not be immediately obvious. There are also events that you must decide whether or not to do.
You will win the game if you defeat the final enemy without your health reaching 0.

Game Story

You are a knight from a far away land seeking your fortune. You have heard tell of a castle with a room full of treasure and enemies against whom you can prove your bravery and skill.
You rode nine days from the nearest town, over mountains, through heavy rain and harsh winds and have finally reached the front gate of the castle.
You adjust your armour, draw your sword and prepare to enter.
